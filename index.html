History Of Sri Vaikuntapuram

Narayana in the form of the saint (600 years ago) and evolving as Vaikuntapuramu Sri Venkateshwara at this holy place.

In south india, there is a ritual which is, where ever, Lord Venkateshwara or Lord Sri Krishna or Sri Rama are installed To describe that Idol we need to understand the song which was written by Annamacharya. That song is “Andariki abhayyammulu ichu cheyi, kanduvagu manchi bangaru cheyi”. As that song describes the lord, even that Idol was evolved with at most beauty with a smiling face, Golden Hand (Abhaya Hastam), Golden Chest Armour, Golden Thirunamam. All devotees who arrived that location were blessed by seeing that beautiful Idol. They were blessed by the luck which couldn’t obtained by other life’s. Then they Installed that Idol in that newly constructed temple. From then the greatness of this temple was spread across the state and county. read more
